<!-- HEADER --> ## Explore a FIS application component pod (Hawtio, logs) These steps demonstrate how a developer can explore the hawtio console of a Fuse Integration Services-based application component running inside a container on OpenShift. 1. Click *Browse*, then *Pods*, then click any pod hosting a FIS application component. 1. Click the small blue *Connect* link. This will take you to the application component Hawtio console. 1. Click *route1* in the tree view, followed by the *Route Diagram* tab to show the camel route graphically. 1. Click other tabs, e.g. *Source* and *Debug* to view the Hawtio functionality. 1. While browsing the Hawtio console, you may wish to have another browser tab open on http://qs-cdi-camel-jetty-xpaas.apps.example.com/camel/hello?name=yourname and refresh it to update the Hawtio statistics. 1. Click the blue *Back* link to return to OpenShift. 1. Click the *Logs* tab. This will show the stdout/stderr of the container. [Back](index.html) <!-- FOOTER -->
