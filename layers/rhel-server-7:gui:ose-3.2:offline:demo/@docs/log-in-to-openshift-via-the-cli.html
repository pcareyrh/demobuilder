<!-- HEADER --> ## Log in to OpenShift via the CLI 1. Double-click the Terminal icon on the desktop. 1. Type `oc login` and press `ENTER` to login to OpenShift. 1. Enter your username (`demo`) and press `ENTER`. 1. Enter the password that you set at login time and press `ENTER`. 1. Type `exit` and press `ENTER` to close the terminal window.
