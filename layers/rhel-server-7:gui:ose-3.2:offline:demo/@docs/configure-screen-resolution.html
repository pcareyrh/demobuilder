<!-- HEADER --> ## Configure screen resolution By default, the demo VM has a screen resolution of 1024x768. If your host supports it, you may want to increase this. If you are connected via VNC, you may be able to increase the screen resolution simply by resizing the VNC window on the host. If this does not work, as an alternative, try the following steps. 1. Navigate to *Applications* / *System Tools* / *Settings*. 1. Click *Displays*. 1. Click *Unknown Display*. 1. Select a suitable resolution from the *Resolution* drop-down menu. 1. Click *Apply*. 1. Click *Keep Changes*. 1. Close the *Displays* window. [Back](index.html) <!-- FOOTER -->
